When it’s no longer just about “what should I decide?”

There are moments when the question is not just about a single decision, but about the feeling that your life as it is no longer matches who you are.

Maybe you’ve changed, grown, or awakened to new parts of yourself, and suddenly your work, routine, relationships, or even where you live don’t fit in the same way.

It doesn’t have to be a crisis. It can be an invitation. But it’s an invitation that can feel overwhelming when walked alone.

What Returning to Yourself is

Returning to Yourself is a 1:1 support for those in transition who want to realign their life — inside and out — with who they are now.

It’s not a formula or a rigid method. It’s an ongoing space of presence, listening and clarification, where we bring into awareness what is already shifting and align your choices with what feels true to you.

We work with what’s alive for you: work, lifestyle, relationships, purpose, moving countries, the way you show up in the world. The topic is not the main thing. You are.

How it can look

This is a deep, ongoing support for those moving through an inner transition and wanting to realign their life, choices and presence with who they are. The structure is clear, but flexible enough to honour your pace.
